How to Mark Your Attendance

Follow these simple steps when you are at the mess:

  1. Open Your Phone's Camera
    Use the standard camera app on your phone.
  2. Point at the Mess QR Code
    The mess provider will have the QR code displayed.
  3. Tap the Link that Appears
    Your phone will show a link. Tapping it will open this website and mark your attendance automatically.
